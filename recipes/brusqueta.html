Brusqueta

Description

Italian recipe, goes well in the afternoon and morning coffee. Easy recipe to make.

Ingredients

  • 50g parmessian cheese
  • Baguette bread of your preference
  • Tomato
  • Oregano
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • basil
  • butter

Steps

  1. Slice the bread in pieces
  2. put some butter on top and bottom of the slices
  3. cut the tomato in little cubes and put then on top of the bread
  4. add some salt on the tomato
  5. spread a lot of parmessian cheese
  6. drizzle with olive oil
  7. the most important part, add the basil
  8. put in the oven until it looks crispy and nice then serve
