Lasagna four cheese

Description

This lasagna takes around 45 minutes and includes parmessian, gouda, provolone and blue cheese with a good lasagna pasta

Ingredients

  • 100g parmessian cheese
  • 80g Gouda cheese
  • 40g blue cheese
  • 30g provolone cheese
  • water
  • salt
  • cream
  • spices
  • lasagna pasta
  • 2 chopped garlic
  • 1/5 sliced onion
  • 1 parsley

Steps

  1. boil the water for 15 minutes and in the last seconds put salt
  2. put the pasta in the boiled water for the time it shows in the packaging
  3. take a pan and put the sliced onion followed by the chopped garlic
  4. add the cream, the spiced and the four cheese respectively while mixing
  5. put the mixture in the bottom of some container to serve then the creamy cheese one by one on top of each slice of lasagna pasta
  6. put all in the oven for 10 minutes with a lot of parmessian on top
  7. when is ready put the parsley and serve
